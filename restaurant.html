Our History

Founded in 1990, our restaurant started as a small family-owned business with a passion for delicious food. Over the years, we have grown into a well-known dining spot, loved for our quality meals and warm hospitality.

Milestones

1990: Opened our first location.
2005: Expanded to multiple cities.
2015: Won "Best Restaurant of the Year" award.
2022: Introduced a new menu with global flavors.
